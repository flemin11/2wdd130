What Not To Sell

Our facility does not accept toys of any kind or furniture type products. We only accept clothing brands and items. Anything that can be considered wearbale. Do not bring worn or broken material as our workers will not give you back money. If you would like to get rid of junk that you don't think will sell, we do have a dump that we can transport it too.

What To Sell

We will take most if not all clothing items that is worn but in a considerable condition. Items can be mailed in and we will asses the quality of the clothing and if it is determined to not be sellable it will be discarded or sent back at your request.
