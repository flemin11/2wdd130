Frequently Asked questions

What makes Nifty Thrifty different from other online shopping websites.

Not only do we provide an easy, user freindy constumer interface, we also allow the customer to get money for the clothes they donate. Most thrift stores are donation only, but we believe that the seller should recieve money for the items they give up and in return can use it to shop and buy new thrify items.

How do you mail in clothes?

Clothes should be mailed in by any means as long as the shipping adresses are all correct. The clothes should be sent to the warehouse. Location: 266 W 3rd S Rexburg, ID. You can also bring your clothes or items into the store located at the same location to get your credit instantly.

What's the average rate for items?

Our team will assess the quality, style, and appearance of the clothes and give you an estimate of what your clothes are worth. No amount is set, it is all determined by our team.

Does it cost for shipping?

Shipping is always free. To mail in your clothes there might be some shipping cost just for the package and to mail it in, but we will accomadate that with the credit you get back in stores.
