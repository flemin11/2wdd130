Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this webiste is to advertise and create a platform where our online Thrifty Nifty company can grow and generate more revenue. We plan to have this web page well known to bring in more customers and make it easier for them to navigate through everything we sell and offer.

Audience

Looking for anyone looking to give up their old clothes, both young and old. The more vintage or thrifty the better. For the consumers, we are gearing towards those who have taken interest in thrifting or just want a cheaper and easier alternative to shopping.
